Everything an Editor Does — Done by AI

StudioAgent applies a full professional editing workflow on every video.

✂

Smart Scene Cuts

Automatically removes long pauses, filler words ("um", "uh"), and off-topic segments. Only the best moments make the final cut.

📚

AI Captions & Subtitles

Word-level transcript from Gemini is converted to styled, burned-in subtitles. Never lose a viewer to silent autoplay again.

🔍

Dynamic Zoom & Pacing

Ken Burns zoom-ins on key statements, slow-motion for dramatic moments, and speed-ramps on low-energy transitions keep viewers engaged.

🔤

Typography Overlays

Key quotes and statistics are pulled from your transcript and rendered as animated text overlays — like a professional motion designer did it.

🌟

Cinematic Color Grading

Automatic color correction and cinematic filter presets (warm, cool, high-contrast, vibrant) are applied based on the video's mood.

🎶

Background Music Mixing

Background music is timed to your scenes and automatically ducked under your voice — no manual keyframing needed.

📺

Dual Format Export

Every job produces both a 1920×1080 landscape cut for YouTube/LinkedIn and a 1080×1920 vertical cut for Reels, Shorts, and TikTok.

📡

Live Progress Updates

A real-time progress feed shows exactly what the AI is doing — analyzing, planning, rendering — at every step of your job.
